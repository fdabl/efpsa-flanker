Explanation

This is a so-called reaction time experiment, which means that you should answer as quickly as possible. The experiment consists of trials, which simply is a row of letters, as seen below. The central letter is the target letter. You should always press the key that corresponds to the target letter. In the example below, this would be H for the first two, and S for the second two.

HHHHHHH

SSSHSSS

SSSSSSS

HHHSHHH

If you have pressed the right key, you get a green O as feedback. However, if you have pressed the wrong key or were too slow, a red X will appear on the screen. The Experiment consists of 96 Trials, which should take about 3 minutes to complete.

There will be a practice trial for you the get a feeling for the experiment. To demonstrate your understanding, please click the heading Explanation above to proceed with the experiment.
